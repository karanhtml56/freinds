About Bir Billing

Bir Billing is known for its paragliding spots and serene landscapes. Perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Billing is situated north of Bir at the distance of 14 km and gateway to the Thamsar Pass trek (4750 mts). Trek lead to most tribal region “Bara Bhangal” of Himachal Pradesh which is 3 days trek and open from July to October only. Bir is mainly a Buddhist town and houses a monastery and a Tibetan handicraft center.
